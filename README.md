# MATHE ADVISORY Design System

MATHE ADVISORY is a legal, risk, insurance, recovery, compliance and business advisory firm supporting SMEs in Cameroon.

This repository presents the early design system and digital product vision of MATHE ADVISORY.

## Design System

The visual identity is based on clarity, trust, professionalism and accessibility.

It includes:
- Brand positioning
- Service categories
- Client document templates
- Social media content structure
- Legal and business advisory communication formats

## Services Covered

- Legal advisory
- Debt recovery
- Risk management
- Insurance and claims management
- Compliance and governance
- HR structuring
- Real estate management
- Administrative support

## Product Vision

The long-term vision is to build a digital platform that helps SMEs manage:
- Legal documents
- Contracts
- Insurance claims
- Debt recovery follow-up
- Compliance tasks
- Risk alerts
- Client files

## Status

This is an early-stage service and design system repository. Code prototypes and product screens will be added progressively.
